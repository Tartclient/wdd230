Bountiful Foods History

Welcome to Bountiful Foods, your one-stop destination for organic, locally grown foods. As a small business based in Carlsbad, California, we take pride in delivering fresh, healthy food options to our customers. Our selection of produce comes directly from local farmers, as well as from national and international suppliers who share our passion for quality and sustainability.

Locations

Our storefront in Carlsbad caters to walk-in traffic, offering a wide variety of fruits and produce. Additionally, our food trucks cover the coastal area from Los Angeles to San Diego, bringing nutritious and delicious choices right to your neighborhood. To further enhance your convenience, we also accept orders over the phone and through our website.

Mission

At Bountiful Foods, our mission is to promote healthy eating habits among the local population and tourists visiting the coastal areas. With a focus on organic bounty and convenience, we offer made-to-order fruit drinks and fruit bags that are perfect for a quick, nutritious snack or meal on the go.

So, come visit us at our Carlsbad storefront, or find one of our food trucks and discover the difference of organic, locally grown foods for yourself. We look forward to serving you!
